College of Science and Mathematics

The College of Science and Mathematics provides students with academically rigorous programs resulting in disciplinary and interdisciplinary bachelor's and master's degrees.

Currently, CSM has four undergraduate offerings, namely, BS Applied Mathematics, BS Biology, BS Computer Science, and BS Food Technology.
